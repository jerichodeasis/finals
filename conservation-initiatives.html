Our Key Conservation Initiatives

We are actively involved in a variety of global and local initiatives to protect our oceans, wildlife, and ecosystems. Below are some of the key areas we focus on:

  • Coral Reef Restoration: Efforts to restore damaged coral reefs through coral planting and protection.
  • Marine Pollution Cleanup: Initiatives to reduce plastic and chemical waste in oceans.
  • Marine Protected Areas: Designating regions as no-go zones for human activity to allow ecosystems to recover.
  • Sustainable Fisheries: Advocating for sustainable fishing practices to prevent overfishing and preserve marine biodiversity.
  • Wildlife Conservation: Protecting endangered marine species such as sea turtles, sharks, and whales.
