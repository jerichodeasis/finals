Key Government Resources for Marine Conservation

Below is a list of government programs, funding opportunities, and initiatives designed to support marine conservation efforts:

  • NOAA Ocean Conservation Programs

    The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) provides various funding and resources for ocean research, restoration, and conservation.

  • USAID Marine Conservation Programs

    The U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) supports marine conservation initiatives worldwide, focusing on biodiversity and coastal protection.

  • UN Environment - Marine Species Report

    UN Environment offers reports and resources on the current state of marine species and habitats, with a focus on policy recommendations for conservation.

  • UK Marine Management Organisation

    The UK government provides resources and guidelines for marine conservation efforts, including marine protected areas and sustainable fishing practices.

  • Pacific Ocean Initiatives

    The Pacific Ocean government initiatives aim to protect ocean health, promote sustainable fisheries, and enhance marine biodiversity in the region.
