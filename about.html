About This Project

SDG Goal: SDG 14 — Life Below Water

Project Title

BlueGuard Marine Watch

Description

BlueGuard Marine Watch is a multi-page advocacy website promoting marine conservation. It features marine site data, photo galleries, and tables showing pollution, reef bleaching, and community restoration projects. This website helps students and communities understand underwater ecosystems and encourages ocean protection.

Workflow (3 Sentences)

  1. Users explore marine sites, search by keywords, and view image-based tables.
  2. JavaScript filters marine data, loads pages, and stores reports in localStorage.
  3. The platform supports SDG 14 by promoting awareness and action toward ocean conservation.
